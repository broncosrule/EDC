Keynote Speaker

Don Sheibenreif, Gartner


Don Sheibenreif is a distinguished vice President, Analyst with Gartners Customer Experience research group. He works with CIO's and IT leaders on how emerging trends and digital business will impact customer experience. He has also been part of the team driving Gartners thought leadership on digital business and digital transformation over the last seven years. Prior joining Gartner, Don spent 27 years as a corporate marketing leader at the Coca-Cola Company, The Quaker Oats Company, and Grainger. Don holds a B.A. in marketing from Depaul University and and earned hisMBA in marketing from the University of Chicago Booth School of Business. A Chicago native, Don and his wife are based in Southern California where he continues to search for a decent hotdog.
